What it is

Atrophy is a paper-clipboard-feeling iPhone app that asks you to identify machine prose, finish unfamiliar sentences under time pressure, report your habits, and write a paragraph from memory. It scores the gap between what you said about yourself and what the writing actually shows.

It is not a clinical instrument. It does not diagnose anything. It is a calibration check for knowledge workers who suspect they are losing the muscle of unaided thought.

What it is not

  • It is not a chatbot. There is no conversational AI inside the app. That is the point.
  • It is not a clinical diagnosis. The brand is medical-deadpan; the math is not.
  • It does not collect data. There is no analytics SDK, no remote server, no tracking. See the privacy policy.
  • It is not free forever. After the assessment, treatment is a $5/month or $45/year subscription. Terms.
