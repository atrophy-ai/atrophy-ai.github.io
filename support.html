Frequently asked

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancellation is handled by Apple, not by us. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Atrophy Pro → Cancel Subscription. Or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions in any browser signed into your Apple ID.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Until that date you keep access to the treatment program; after, you keep access to the diagnostic.

I subscribed on another device. How do I restore purchases?

Open Atrophy on the new device, navigate to the paywall (after taking the diagnostic), and tap Restore purchases at the bottom. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, your active subscription will reactivate within seconds.

Can I export my data?

There is nothing to export. Atrophy stores almost nothing on your device and nothing on a server. Each diagnostic resets when you close the app. The treatment program tracks only your enrollment date locally. See the privacy policy for details.

I'd like a refund.

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com. We have no ability to issue refunds directly because we never see your payment information.

Accessibility.

Atrophy is built in native SwiftUI, which inherits VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion, and the Smart Invert color modes by default. If you find a screen that doesn't respond to your accessibility settings the way you expect, please report it via the contact channels above — accessibility regressions are bugs, not edge cases.

I'm a journalist / researcher / clinician.

The methodology is described in plain English on the methodology page, with the convergent literatures the construct is built on. The full research synthesis (sources, citations, anti-claims) is in a document available on request — email and we'll send it.

Why won't you put a chatbot in the app?

That is the entire point. Atrophy exists to give knowledge workers a five-minute window without one. If we shipped an AI helper, we'd be cosplaying as the problem.

Crisis

If you are in distress

Atrophy is not a clinical tool. If you are in mental-health distress, please contact a real crisis service:

  • United States — 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). Call or text.
  • United Kingdom & Ireland — Samaritans, 116 123. Free.
  • Australia — Lifeline, 13 11 14.
  • Other countries — see the findahelpline.com directory.
